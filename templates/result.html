1. Coloring Options

1. Press "1" - Color protein by different side chain

2. Press "2" - Color protein by different atom

3. Press "3" - Color protein by differing regions from sequence alignment

4. Press "4" - Color protein by default rendering of protein

5. Press "F1" - Color protein by rendering of protein 1

6. Press "F2" - Color protein by rendering of protein 2

7. Press "F3" - Color protein by kabsch Algorithm

2, Camera Controls

Use the following keys to control the camera:

  • Press "W" to move the camera up
  • Press "S" to move the camera down
  • Press "A" to move the camera left
  • Press "D" to move the camera right

3. Zoom Controls

Use the mouse scroll wheel to:

  • Scroll forward to zoom in
  • Scroll backward to zoom out

Mouse Movement for Rotation

You can rotate the view by moving the mouse:

  • Move the mouse up to rotate upwards
  • Move the mouse down to rotate downwards
  • Move the mouse left to rotate left
  • Move the mouse right to rotate right
